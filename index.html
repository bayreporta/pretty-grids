Family Child Care Facilities

Between 2006 and 2012, 84 percent of California counties — 49 total — saw a decline in the number of licensed familiy child care facilities. Only three counties — Santa Clara, Santa Cruz and Tulare — saw an increase.

Child Care Centers

Between 2006 and 2012, 50 percent of California counties — 29 total — saw an increase in the number of licensed child care centers, while 36 percent — 21 total — saw a decline.
